Popular Places in Nepal

Chitwan National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Chitwan National Park is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including rhinos, tigers, and elephants, and offers jungle safaris.

Lumbini

Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, is an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists, with temples, monasteries, and the sacred Bodhi tree.

Annapurna Circuit

The Annapurna Circuit is a famous trekking route, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas and diverse landscapes, from subtropical forests to snow-capped peaks.
